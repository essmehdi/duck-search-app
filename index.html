DuckDuckGo default themes support

DuckDuckGo search engine comes with 4 search themes: Default, Basic, Gray and Black. This app supports them natively to ensure a more unified search experience. More themes are coming and it's planned to make it more customizable in the future so stay tuned for that !

Local search history

The web version of the search engine doesn't have a search history feature, so you can find it in this app. Your search queries are saved in your device storage in local database. You can choose to disable it in the app settings.

Improved DuckDuckGo mobile search experience

DuckDuckGo Search & Widget provides an enhanced experience of search combining between DuckDuckGo privacy respect and near-Google app experience. With this app, you can make your search queries on DuckDuckGo faster and more efficient than ever.
